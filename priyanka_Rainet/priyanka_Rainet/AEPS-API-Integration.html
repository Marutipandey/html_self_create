What is AEPS, and How Does it Work?

AEPS is an Aadhaar-enabled payment system that allows you to perform a range of financial transactions with ease. These include making payments, interbank or intrabank money transfers, withdrawals, deposits, checking your bank balance, and more. AEPS is a simple, secure, and user-friendly platform that simplifies all your financial transactions.

The AEPS works through MicroATM terminals that are connected to the Aadhaar authentication server. When a customer initiates a transaction, the business correspondent of any bank verifies the customer's identity using Aadhaar authentication. Once the authentication is successful, the transaction is processed, and the customer's account is debited or credited accordingly.
